Volando A Nuevas Alturas Merchandise Line

February 2023

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

"Volando a Nuevas Alturas" is the 2023 Chicanx/Latinx Collegiate Association merchandise line, emphasizing the rich heritage of chicanx and latinx culture. Inspired by the 1970s psychedelic art movement, this merchandise line hopes to break free from the eurocentric design aesthetics and inspire individuals to "soar to new heights". The merchandise line consists of t-shirts, long sleeves, crew necks, and hoodies.
