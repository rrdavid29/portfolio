Push Me Under: Hear My Cry

June 2023

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign

"Push Me Under: Hear My Cry", written by Bianca Moazez, highlights the injustice faced by women in Iran due to the morality police. This design spread aims to elevate the poem by using elements from traditional culturally Iranian rugs and the importance of the hijab and hair to Iranian women.
