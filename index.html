About Me

Hi! My name is Ronnie David. As a recent graduate from the University of California, Davis with a Bachelor of Arts in Design and a Bachelor of Science in Cognitive Science, I hope to use a dynamic blend of creativity and scientific understanding to influence my design.

My degree in Design helped me develop my skills in visual communication, problem solving, and creating captivating spaces, while my Cognitive Science degree provided an understanding of human cognition, perception, and behavior. The blend between these studies allowed me to focus on aesthetics, as well as how individuals perceive and interact with the outside world.

As I embark on the next chapter of my career, I am excited to continue further develop my skills and push the boundaries of design. Thank you for taking the time to look through my portfolio!
